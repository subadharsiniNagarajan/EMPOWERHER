# 👩‍💼 EmpowerHer - User Guide

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Open the Application
1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. You'll see the beautiful landing page with the EmpowerHer logo

### Step 2: Select Your Language
1. Choose from 6 available languages:
   - English
   - தமிழ் (Tamil)
   - हिंदी (Hindi)
   - తెలుగు (Telugu)
   - ಕನ್ನಡ (Kannada)
   - മലയാളം (Malayalam)
2. Click on your preferred language button
3. The interface will update immediately

### Step 3: Enter Your Name
1. Type your name in the input field
2. This personalizes your experience throughout the app

### Step 4: Start Your Journey
1. Click the "Start My Journey 🚀" button
2. You'll be taken to the main dashboard

## Feature Guide

### 1. Ask AI Assistant 🎤

**What it does:**
- Provides instant business advice
- Answers questions about different businesses
- Gives investment and income estimates

**How to use:**
1. Click "Ask AI" card
2. Type your question in the chat box
3. Press Send or hit Enter
4. Get detailed business information

**Tips:**
- Ask about specific businesses (e.g., "cooking", "tailoring")
- Use the microphone button for voice input
- Ask follow-up questions for more details

**Example Questions:**
- "Tell me about baking business"
- "How to start mehendi business?"
- "What is the investment for yoga classes?"

### 2. Business Guide 💡

**What it does:**
- Creates customized business plans
- Calculates profit projections
- Provides step-by-step guidance

**How to use:**
1. Click "Business Guide" card
2. Select your skill from dropdown (50+ options!)
3. Enter your investment budget
4. Enter hours you can work daily
5. Choose your operating location
6. Click "Generate My Business Plan"

**What you get:**
- Step-by-step startup guide
- Investment breakdown
- Pricing strategy
- Monthly profit projections
- Marketing plan
- Success tips

**Example:**
```
Skill: Baking
Budget: ₹15,000
Time: 4 hours/day
Location: From Home
Result: Detailed plan with ₹20,000-60,000 monthly income potential
```

### 3. Profit Calculator 💰

**What it does:**
- Calculates your monthly profit
- Shows revenue vs expenses
- Provides profit margin analysis

**How to use:**
1. Click "Profit Calculator" card
2. Enter cost per product/service
3. Enter your selling price
4. Enter monthly sales quantity
5. Click "Calculate My Profit"

**What you get:**
- Total monthly revenue
- Total monthly expenses
- Monthly profit
- Profit per unit
- Profit margin percentage
- Tips to increase profit

**Example:**
```
Cost: ₹50
Selling Price: ₹100
Quantity: 200/month
Result: ₹10,000 monthly profit (50% margin)
```

- Use simple sentences

- Requires internet connection
- Not all browsers support all languages

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Yes! Once loaded, most features work offline. Voice input requires internet.

**Q: Is my data saved?**
A: Yes, your name and language preference are saved in your browser.

**Q: Can I change language later?**
A: Yes! Use the language dropdown in the dashboard header.

**Q: Are the income estimates realistic?**
A: Yes, based on real homemaker businesses. Actual income varies.

**Q: Can I start with less investment?**
A: Yes! Start small and grow gradually. Many businesses need minimal investment.

**Q: Do I need technical knowledge?**
A: No! The app is designed for complete beginners.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: Yes! Fully responsive and works great on phones.

**Q: How do I print my business plan?**
A: Use browser's print function (Ctrl+P) or take screenshots.
